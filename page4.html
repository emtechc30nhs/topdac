      I and my classmate together with my older brother planned to have a tour in the northern blossom flower farm. Our purpose is to fulfill the requirement of our subject in Em tech, creating a web page, and at the same time having an adventure. My brother serves as our driver. For the gasoline we contributed money of a total of 300 pesos.

        We departed from Abiang at 2:00 pm and arrived at 2:40 pm in our destination. We traveled 12 kilometers to reach our destination. From Halsema highway there is another road going to the farm, a length of 100 meters. Upon arriving at the parking area of the owner we were welcomed by the tourist guide. We entered into the owner’s house and signed in their logbook. We paid 200 pesos per head as entrance fee. There are other tourists who came as well.

         The tourist guide accompanied us to view the plants in the farm and gave explanation about the flowers. We took pictures. The tourist guide helps us to take pictures of us positioning in the center of the garden. When taking a picture be careful not to step in flower plants. It took about 1 hour and 30 minutes to roam around the farm

        Sadly, we have to go home. After the viewing, we returned at the house. Before leaving, they served us hot Arabica coffee and a cinnamon bread because it was cold there.We take this time to have an interview to the owner and, gladly, she granted our request despite of how busy she is.

     Before going home, we didn’t leave the chance to visit the highest point so we took a ride for 3 kilometer. When we came at that place there are many tourists who are viewing the scenery and taking pictures.

        On our way home, we stopped at the half tunnel to take pictures from it.

        It was already dusk but still we are on the way until it became dark.It was too way long to drive.I hope there are Inns there to sleep at night but none. 6:00 pm When we had already arrived home.

        The trip was really a great experience because it was rare to have this because of school life. For this coming vacation I hope there will be more tours to come.
